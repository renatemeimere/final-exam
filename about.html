About me

In my teenage years I forgot about my dreams and let some dumb stereotypes take an advantage of me. For example “money will make you happy”, “you should work with something economics related, because that is where the money is”. Thus - I realised that I was living the life of someone else by fulfilling their goals instead of aiming for mine. And that was enough.

By summer 2018 I decided to start a new chapter in my life. I decided to set my own goals and do what I love. This led me back to graphical design.

Currently I am studying multimedia design and working as a freelance photographer. I love what I do. And I am happy to see when my work is spreading happiness and joy. I have found my passion for sure. Here is where I will publish the things I have done so far. Stay tuned!
